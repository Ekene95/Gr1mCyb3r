About Me

Hello! I'm Ekene Ejike, a passionate and results-driven DevSecOps Engineer with a strong background in IT Infrastructure, Securuty and Technical support. My journey in tech is driven by a deep curiosity for building secure, scalable, and efficient software systems.

I specialize in integrating security into every phase of the CI/CD pipeline, often referred to as "shifting left." This includes implementing static and dynamic application security testing (SAST, DAST), software composition analysis (SCA), and ensuring software supply chain integrity through tools like Sigstore Cosign. I believe that security should be an integral part of development, not an afterthought.

Beyond security, I enjoy crafting robust web applications using modern front-end technologies and back-end frameworks. This portfolio is a space where I share my learnings, showcase my projects, and contribute to the vibrant DevSecOps and development community.

I'm always eager to learn new technologies and explore innovative solutions to complex problems. Feel free to connect with me through my contact page or explore my work on GitHub ↗.
